# The Toasted Mallow — Ingredient Cost Comparison Report
**Prepared:** April 2026  
**Purpose:** Retail vs. wholesale pricing analysis for cost-reduction toolkit  
**Scope:** Core marshmallow + cupcake ingredients, packaging, and alternative supplier options

---

## Notes on Methodology

- Retail prices sourced from Walmart, Kroger, Target, and BLS/FRED average retail data (current as of early 2026)
- Wholesale prices sourced from WebstaurantStore (verified live pricing), Costco Business, and Amazon bulk listings
- Cost-per-lb or cost-per-oz normalized for apples-to-apples comparison
- "Savings %" reflects the difference between retail $/lb and best wholesale $/lb
- All prices verified April 2026 unless noted

## LGBTQ+, Women-Owned, and Arizona-Local Options

### Arizona Local / Regional

**US Foods CHEF'STORE — Tempe, AZ**  
3115 S McClintock Dr, Tempe, AZ 85282 | (480) 838-1199  
Mon–Sat 7am–6pm, Sun 8am–4pm  
Open to the public (no membership required). Stocks 50-lb flour, bulk dairy, commercial baking supplies. **Closest to Gilbert, AZ of any foodservice warehouse.** Worth a visit before committing to an online wholesale account — same-day pickup saves shipping costs on heavy items.

**Phoenix Food Co-Op**  
Community-owned cooperative grocer in Phoenix. Focuses on locally produced and accessible food. Not a wholesale supplier but may carry local/artisan ingredient brands at member pricing, and may offer purchasing network connections.

**Arizona food-direct options:**  
The state has several small-farm cooperatives through LocalHarvest and Devour Good Food's AZ food co-op directory. These are retail/CSA-focused, not bulk ingredient suppliers, but can be useful for differentiated ingredients (local honey, specialty flavors).

### Women-Owned / LGBTQ+-Owned Ingredient Suppliers (National, Ship to AZ)

No verified LGBTQ+-owned or women-owned bulk commodity ingredient distributor (for sugar, flour, gelatin at industrial scale) was found in current research. The certified-supplier landscape for commodity baking ingredients is dominated by large agribusiness.

**However — two meaningful options:**

1. **Diaspora Co.** (BIPOC + LGBTQ+ led) — High-quality single-origin spices and specialty flavors. Not commodity but excellent for signature flavoring differentiators (cardamom, turmeric, specialty chili). diasporaco.com

2. **Bakers Authority** — Not LGBTQ-owned, but a women-founded specialty baking wholesaler with strong community values, competitive bulk pricing on Domino sugar, gelatin, and specialty ingredients. bakersauthority.com

3. **Certification programs to ask suppliers about:**  
   - WBENC (Women's Business Enterprise National Council) — major distributors like US Foods have WBENC-certified divisions
   - NGLCC (National LGBT Chamber of Commerce) supplier directory at nglcc.org — search for food & beverage members

## Priority Recommendations for Immediate Savings

1. **Gel food coloring** — Switch from Wilton retail to Chefmaster 10.5 oz or 1-gal at WebstaurantStore. **80% cost reduction per oz.**

2. **Gelatin** — Move from Knox retail packets to WebstaurantStore 50-lb commercial blocks. **85–88% savings.** For kosher-certified product, contact FIT Gelatins (Denver) for a quote on 55-lb bags.

3. **Vanilla and flavor extracts** — Move from McCormick 2 oz retail to LorAnn 16 oz at WebstaurantStore. **~48% savings per oz.**

4. **Powdered sugar** — 50 lb bags at $1.04/lb vs. $2.40/lb branded retail. **57% savings** — especially impactful since marshmallow and cupcake frosting use large volumes.

5. **Packaging** — Eliminate Hobby Lobby and Michaels as packaging sources entirely. WebstaurantStore case pricing saves 30–43% on boxes; Nashville Wraps or ULINE saves 85–92% on tissue paper.

6. **Visit CHEF'STORE Tempe** — Avoid shipping costs on heavy commodities (flour, sugar, butter). Restaurant-supply warehouse pickup near Gilbert is cost-effective for weekly or biweekly runs.

7. **WebstaurantStore Plus membership** — At $99/year, the discounted cream pricing alone pays for itself at moderate volume, and heavy cream usage for cupcake frosting is high.
